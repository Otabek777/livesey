Who is Dr. Livesey?

Dr. Livesey is a character from the 1988 Soviet animated film Treasure Island. Intelligent, calm, always with perfect posture and a subtle smile — he became a symbol of confidence, self-control, and inner strength.

The Meme

In 2022, a scene of Dr. Livesey walking with unbeatable posture and a smile, synced to phonk music, went viral on TikTok and YouTube. That’s how the Dr. Livesey Phonk Walk meme was born — a symbol of sigma energy and ironic charisma. Millions of views, countless remixes, and internet legend status followed.

The Coin Launch

Dr. Livesey Phonk Walk Coin is the natural evolution of the meme — a memecoin inspired by calm power and confident forward motion. The coin quickly gained popularity in crypto communities and social media, becoming a symbol of style, irony, and unshakable energy.
